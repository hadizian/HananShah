About

Hanan Shah

Hanan Shah is an independent singer and performer from Kerala, India, known for his soulful voice and raw emotional depth. Blending Malayalam roots with global influences, Hanan’s music bridges cultures and feelings — from heartfelt ballads to electrifying experimental sounds. Driven by passion and authenticity, he has grown a loyal audience through self-produced tracks, creative visuals, and a unique vibe that defies trends. Whether he’s singing about love, identity, or life’s quiet moments, Hanan delivers music that feels personal and powerful. Currently building his catalog across platforms like Spotify, YouTube, and Instagram, Hanan Shah is not just a voice — he’s a movement in the making.
